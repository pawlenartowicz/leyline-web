# Contributing to Leyline

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

By submitting a pull request to this repository, you agree to the [Leyline Contributor License Agreement](https://github.com/pawlenartowicz/cla/blob/main/CLA.md). On your first PR the [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io/) bot will guide you through signing; a single signature covers all Leyline repositories.
